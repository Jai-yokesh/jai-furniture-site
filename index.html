About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1982, Jai Furniture is a proud family-run business with a legacy of fine craftsmanship. We specialize in handcrafted teak wood furniture, combining traditional skills with simple, timeless design. Every piece is made by skilled artisans using durable, high-quality wood—built to last and meant to be passed down through generations..

Our Services

Custom Wood Furniture

We design and craft custom furniture pieces tailored to your specific needs and preferences. From concept to creation, we work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

  • Personalized design consultation
  • Selection of premium hardwoods
  • Handcrafted by master artisans

Furniture Repair

Restore your beloved wooden furniture to its former glory. Our skilled craftsmen can repair, refinish, and rejuvenate your cherished pieces.

  • Structural repairs and reinforcement
  • Surface refinishing and restoration
  • Replacement of damaged components

Interior Woodwork

Transform your home with custom interior woodwork. From wall paneling to ceiling designs, we create architectural elements that add character and warmth.

  • Custom wall paneling and wainscoting
  • Decorative ceiling beams and coffers
  • Built-in cabinetry and shelving

Restoration Projects

Specializing in the restoration of antique and heritage wooden furniture and architectural elements, preserving their historical value and beauty.

  • Antique furniture restoration
  • Heritage building elements preservation
  • Period-appropriate techniques and materials
